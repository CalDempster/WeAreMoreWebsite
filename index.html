Welcome to MORE

A pioneering initiative focused on making original recycled equipment.

At MORE, we strive to create a greener and more accessible workplace for the chronically underrepresented women in the tech landscape.
Our mission is to develop an alternate pathway for electronics, instead of tech to landfill, by creating upcycled products.

Overview

Derry has historically been a city of working women. From the linen factories to the home, it was women who kept the city running, but in the evolving workplace women have been left behind in tech.

Our journey began at Generation Innovation and focused on reducing e-waste , but it has evolved to so much more and we hope you join us on pushing for this workplace change.
