Welcome to MORE

A pioneering initiative dedicated to empowering women of all ages through the innovative use of upcycled technology.

At MORE, we believe in the transformative power of information and communication technology (ICT) and are committed to making it accessible to everyone, women who have been historically underrepresented in the tech industry.Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by providing comprehensive ICT education using repurposed computers and electronic scrap.

Our Journey

MORE originally started as an issue of reducing waste but then it became so much more.I hope you will help us on our journey of innovation.

The ten of us along with our brilliant mentors at fujitsu and Generation innovation began tackling issues of waste , upcycling and inclusion.
