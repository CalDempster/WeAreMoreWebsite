Welcome to MORE

A pioneering initiative focused on making original recycled equipment.

At MORE, we strive to create a greener and more accessible workplace for the chronically underrepresented women in the tech landscape.
Our mission is to develop an alternate pathway for electronics, instead of tech to landfill, by creating upcycled products. At More, we believe in empowering women as a crucial part of our mission. By partnering with female artisans and entrepreneurs, we not only provide opportunities for skill development and economic independence but also amplify their voices in the sustainability movement. Our commitment extends beyond upcycling; we aim to create a supportive community that champions women's creativity and innovation. Together, we can transform waste into beautiful products while uplifting women and fostering a brighter, more equitable future for all.
